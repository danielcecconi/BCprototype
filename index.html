Who We Are:

At Bazaar Ceramics our ambition is to produce the highest quality ceramic wares for both the home as well national and international collectors. Bazaar Ceramics has been operating for over 20 years. Starting as a small collective in the township of Hahndorf, South Australia, Bazaar Ceramics has grown consistantly to become the company it is today. We pride ourselves in the manufacture of ceramic ware that is sought after around the world. We welcome you to our website and would be delighted to have you look around.
